10-slide pitch deck text — AgroSense Agent

Slide 1 — Title
AgroSense Agent — Market Insights & Climate Advisor for Smallholder Farmers

Slide 2 — Problem
Farmers lack real-time local market data, buyer access, and timely weather alerts — this causes income loss and waste.

Slide 3 — Solution
A lightweight AI assistant that delivers market prices, 7-day forecasts, weather alerts, and buyer suggestions in English and Hausa.

Slide 4 — Technology
React + Vite frontend, Node/Express backend, optional Azure OpenAI, JSON-based mock data for offline demos.

Slide 5 — How it works
1) Frontend queries backend 2) ForecastController returns rule-based forecasts 3) OpenAIService returns chat replies (mock or live)

Slide 6 — Demo highlights
Live mock demo: price query, Hausa voice input, forecast + buyer suggestion.

Slide 7 — Impact
Potential to increase farmer income by 20–30% in pilot areas; improves market transparency and reduces exploitation.

Slide 8 — Business model
Freemium access, premium analytics for cooperatives, integration partnerships with buyers & aggregators.

Slide 9 — Roadmap
Add WhatsApp/SMS integration, memory & personalization, map/GPS, image inputs for crop quality.
